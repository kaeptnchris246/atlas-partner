Your Dotiva Sales Cockpit

Exactly how the work looks — day by day

Dotiva is your sales cockpit: all your customers, messages, tasks and earnings in one simple screen. Aurelia works inside it for you. Here is exactly what you do:

📱A phone is enough. You can run everything from your mobile — a laptop is nice for bigger tasks but not required.
Open Dotiva in the morning

On your phone you see today's tasks: who to contact, who replied, what to follow up.

Add a business you met (30 seconds)

Name + LINE/phone. That's all — you met them at a shop, market or through friends.

ATLAS researches, Aurelia writes

ATLAS looks up the business; Aurelia drafts a friendly first message for you automatically.

You read & tap "approve"

You just check the message and approve — Aurelia sends it (LINE/email) and follows up on its own.

Ping when they reply

Dotiva pings you when a customer answers. You step in for the human conversation.

Show the demo & agree a price

Open the live demo on your phone, show them, agree a price you're happy with.

Tap "won" → we build → you get paid

We build the product; Dotiva tracks the deal and shows your earnings clearly.

So what do YOU actually do?

  • Talk to people (in person or on LINE) — the part humans do best.
  • Check & approve the messages Aurelia writes.
  • Show a demo and close the deal.
  • Everything technical — research, writing, follow-up, building — is automated.
